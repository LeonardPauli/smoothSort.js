Sort things out smoothly.

Adds smooth drag-n-drop sorting to any HTML list with a simple call.

  • View & fork the code on GitHub, or drag me!
  • You can also drag here and do:   $ bower install smooth-sort
  • It does currently requires jQuery, and some css, but other than that, it's just a simple call between you and that smoothness: 
  •     $('ul').smoothSort();
